Bannana Pudding

Hand made Bannana Pudding

Bannana pudding is a nice dessert to make for the family after a nice dinner meal, special for all celebrations.

Ingridients:

  • Bannanas
  • Nilla Wafers
  • Puddding Mix
  • Cool Whip

Steps:

  1. Mix Cool whip and Pudding mix
  2. Place cut bannanas on top of the pan
  3. Chill in fridge for 30 minutes before serving
